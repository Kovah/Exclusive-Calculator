Platform Exclusive Calculator

With this calculator a publisher / developer can find out if he should release his game as an exclusive for only one publishing platform or not.

  1. Choose your primary platform you want to go exclusive with.
  2. Enter both the amount of expected sales and your initial sales price for this platform.
  3. Select at least one other platform and enter an expected sales amount for this platform.
  4. Press calculate.
